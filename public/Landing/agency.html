How to start

Start experiencing the barter experience right now.

Create an account

  • It's easy an quick.
  • You can do it with your social account. This will give you credibility in the community.

Create your account

Upload your Sugar

  • Any item you may have works.
  • Games, books, stuff you don't longer use, everything.
  • Or any service you can offer.
  • This is things you can imagine that you are good at (or at least are wiling to do)

Barter!

  • Check the other users interest hit barter and that's it!
  • You can also find a match with the match tool.
