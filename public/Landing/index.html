Welcome to the barter revolution

It has never been so easy to get what you want

Easy to Use

With a few clicks you can do everything: upload items or services (sugars), browse what people are offering and barter with them.

Community

Discover who is offering sugars near you by filtering by distance. With different tools we'll help you find the perfect match!

Security

Through a variety of validation methods, we offer a community of trust and reliability so you know who you are bartering with.

Built with Love

This marketplace was born in the community and for the community with the intention of helping you to get what you want.

Why wait around for a paycheck to solve all of your problems? Join barter sugar and start using resources that you have to trade for what you need today!
Start Bartering!

How to start

Start trading in three easy steps.

Create an account

  • It’s easy and quick.
  • You can sign in with a social account, giving you credibility in the community.

Create your account

Upload your Sugar

  • Any item or service you are willing to offer.
  • Items can be: books, games, sports equipment, you name it!
  • Services can be: haircuts, moving help, tutoring, music lessons, your imagination is the limit!

Barter!

  • Start a barter by clicking on the “barter” button on the sugar you want.
  • Use our special Find Match feature to find the perfect match!

Start Bartering!
